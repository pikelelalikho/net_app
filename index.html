About Me

I am a cybersecurity and full-stack development engineer from Cape Town, South Africa, with hands-on experience in cybersecurity operations, Linux server administration, cloud infrastructure, and enterprise application deployments.

My journey began after completing Cybersecurity Fundamentals and Cybersecurity Defence Toolbox training with Digital Regenesys, where I built a strong foundation in digital defense and threat analysis. This period sparked my interest in programming, leading me to teach myself web development, automation, and software engineering alongside my security studies.

I gained practical industry experience during my Incident Response and Digital Forensics internship at the Center for Cyber Security Studies and Research, working on network analysis, vulnerability assessments, digital investigations, and real-world incident response workflows.

Over time I expanded into full-stack development and infrastructure engineering — building and deploying enterprise platforms on Ubuntu Linux with Cloudflare Tunnel, PM2, NGINX, JWT authentication, and Socket.IO real-time systems.

I also completed the CAPACITI AI Bootcamp, exploring AI systems, prompt engineering, ethical AI, and intelligent automation. I continue growing toward senior cybersecurity engineering, ethical hacking, and scalable infrastructure design.
